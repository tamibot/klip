What's inside

Everything the copy-paste loop needs.

One native menu-bar app for the whole flow — capture it, read it, say it, clean it, and keep your keys safe.

Searchable history

⌥⇧E and your last hundred clips — text and images — are a keystroke away. Instant search, type filters, full keyboard navigation.

Capture & annotate

⌥⇧D — drag over a frozen screen, then mark it up: arrows, text, blur, spotlight, counters, undo/redo. Lands in history and on the clipboard, confirmed by a notification — never by a panel opening over your work.

Text recognition

⌥⇧F — select any region and its text is OCR'd straight to the clipboard with Apple's Vision engine. On-device, with a toast to confirm.

Voice & video → text

⌥⇧R to dictate, or ⌥⇧O to drop audio & video. Transcribed on your Mac by Whisper — no key, no account, no upload.

Meeting notes, no bot

⌥⇧M on any call — Zoom, Meet, Teams, FaceTime. Records your mic + the system audio (nobody joins) and transcribes it on-device, Me / Them labelled.

Paste-ready, always clean

One click reformats a clip for WhatsApp, rich email, Markdown or a fenced code block (⌘↩) — and every rich copy is stored clean: bold and emojis kept, backgrounds and fonts dropped.

Record screen → video/GIF

⌥⇧V — select a region (or record the full screen) with system audio; a floating frame marks what is being recorded. It lands in your history: play, save, or one-tap convert to GIF.

Scrolling capture

⌥⇧S — pick the content area and Klip scrolls the page for you: back to the top first, then all the way down, stitching as it goes. One tall screenshot of the whole page.

Encrypted credentials

Tokens and API keys are auto-detected, shown masked, encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM, key in the Keychain) and never auto-pasted.

Muscle memory

Seven global shortcuts. One hand.

Grouped on ⌥⇧ (Option+Shift) on the left of the keyboard — comfortable to hold, and rarely claimed by your editor or browser.

⌥⇧EHistory
⌥⇧RVoice note
⌥⇧DAnnotate
⌥⇧FOCR text
⌥⇧OUpload media
⌥⇧MMeeting
⌥⇧VRecord

All seven are configurable in Preferences › Shortcuts. Inside the panel: ↑↓ to move, ↩ to paste, ⌘↩ to copy as a code block, Esc to close.

On-device, always

Private by design

Your clipboard is nobody's business but yours.

Klip is local-only. Your history, images and audio live on your Mac with 0600 permissions — and stay there. There is no cloud to opt into: no accounts, no sync, no service to hand your clips to. Klip has nowhere to send them.

  • Encrypted at restDetected keys and tokens are AES-256-GCM encrypted, with the key in the macOS Keychain — never written to items.json or backups in the clear.
  • On-device transcriptionVoice, video and meeting notes run offline with Whisper — the only engine Klip has. The model is downloaded once; your audio never goes the other way.
  • No telemetry, everNo analytics, no update pings, no accounts. Klip ignores password-manager content and lets you exclude specific apps.
0 KBof your data leaves the Mac. No exceptions, no opt-outs to check.

Get Klip

Build from source in a minute.

No full Xcode required — just the Command Line Tools. The script builds, signs, installs to Applications and launches it.

zsh — klip
# Command Line Tools (if you don't have them)
xcode-select --install

# Clone & install
git clone https://github.com/tamibot/klip.git klip
cd klip
./install.sh

The 📋 icon lands in your menu bar — press ⌥⇧E to open the history. macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later, Intel or Apple Silicon. A prebuilt download will arrive on GitHub releases; a Windows version is planned.
